Who I am

In a nutshell I would consider myself a lifelong learner. I get enjoyment from learning as much as I can today for that day when someone asks for my help in the future. In college I studied Aerospace Engineering and I worked on some pretty cool projects and teams prototyping, testing, and shipping real products out there in the world today. Nowadays what I get my enjoyment from is making something from just an idea with code.

I'm happy as long as there is a problem to solve and even happier if I get to teach myself something new solving it. Even after long days at work I found myself wanting to do more, wanting to learn more to be more precise, so I could do my job more effectively or just to pick up a new skill/hobby. So I started teaching myself to program with Python.

Once I started I got hooked! Learning Python led to teaching myself HTML, CSS, Javascript, SQL, and frameworks like Django. All of which were used to build this website/blog. And of course, coming from Aerospace Design I have experience with 3D modeling software.

My goals for the future are to continue to do meaningful work, learn every day, and I hope to one day share all that knowledge aspiring developers that come after me or team members at my next job.
